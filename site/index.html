Cross definitions

Signals are detected on the latest trading day only. The moving averages must cross between the prior session and today.

golden
Golden Cross: the 50-day exponential moving average (EMA) crosses above the 200-day EMA. Often interpreted as a longer-term bullish trend shift.
death
Death Cross: the 50-day EMA crosses below the 200-day EMA. Often interpreted as a longer-term bearish trend shift.
golden_st
Short Term Golden Cross: the 21-day EMA crosses above the 50-day EMA. A faster signal using shorter moving averages.
death_st
Short Term Death Cross: the 21-day EMA crosses below the 50-day EMA. A faster bearish cross on the 21/50 EMA pair.
